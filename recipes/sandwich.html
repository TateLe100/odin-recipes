Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Basic PB&J recipe for the a great start to a early morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
  • 2 tablespoons jelly or jam (grape, strawberry, or your favorite flavor)
Instructions:
  1. Spread the Peanut Butter:
    • Lay out the two slices of bread.
    • Spread 2 tablespoons of peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread the Jelly:
    • Spread 2 tablespoons of jelly or jam evenly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich:
    • Place the slice with peanut butter on top of the slice with jelly, peanut butter side facing down.
  4. Serve:
    • Cut the sandwich in half, diagonally or horizontally, if desired.
